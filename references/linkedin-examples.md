# LinkedIn Outreach Examples

## Style Principles (derived from examples below)

- Open with what caught your eye about the specific role — show you read it
- Lead with the most relevant/surprising credential first, not a generic intro
- One concrete achievement or data point per paragraph max
- Keep it under 150 words; short messages get read
- Always end with a low-ask CTA ("would love to connect", "happy to chat briefly")
- Sign off with name, email, and phone every time
- Tone: warm but direct — no filler phrases like "I hope this finds you well"
