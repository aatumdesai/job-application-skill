## The honest diagnosis of the fast rejections

Bullet quality is **not** the main driver — the resume's bullets are already strong. The
fast rejections are mostly **profile-screen**, in priority order:

1. **Pedigree screen.** 6 of 10 explicitly want management consulting / IB / PE / BizOps.
   He has real-estate investment ops + MBA. A recruiter Boolean for "McKinsey/Bain/Goldman/
   BizOps" filters him out before the bullets are ever read. → counter by making PINN read
   as **"Strategy & Operations / BizOps"**, not real estate finance.
2. **Years-in-title screen.** Square (8-12), Affirm (8+), Retool/Plaid (6-8/6-11) likely
   auto-reject on tenure — "Business Strategy & Insights Manager" reads as ~9 months. →
   counter by reframing PINN tenure as relevant strategy/ops (≈5 yrs), and **stop applying
   to 8+ roles** unless there's a referral.
3. **Industry screen.** fintech (Plaid/Affirm), healthcare (Hinge), marketplace (Whatnot). →
   lean Abbott for healthcare, PINN capital-allocation for fintech-adjacent financial fluency.
4. **Missing keyword clusters** — operating rhythm (MBR/QBR), market sizing/pilots,
   explicit "internal consultant / structured problem-solving."
