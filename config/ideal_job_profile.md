# Ideal Job Profile — Aatum Desai

## Role Type
Product-adjacent Strategy & Operations or Product Strategy position.

## What the Role Does
- Drives decisions on roadmap prioritization, monetization, and GTM strategy using rigorous analytics
- Operates as an internal strategic partner to Product and executive leadership
- Owns ambiguous, high-impact problems rather than execution-heavy GTM operations
- Influences product direction, not just reporting on it

## Target Environment
- High-prestige tech company (Google, Stripe, Netflix, Apple, OpenAI, Airbnb, etc.)
- OR top-tier, high-growth startup (Series B–D, strong investors, clear category leadership)
- Strong compensation and career leverage
- Clear path to senior/director-level influence

## Strong Signals (score UP for these)
- Title contains: Strategy, Product Strategy, Product Operations, Business Strategy, Insights, Planning
- Role explicitly involves cross-functional work with Product, Engineering, or C-suite
- Role involves analytics, data-driven decision-making, or prioritization frameworks
- Role at a recognized prestige company or well-funded startup
- Seniority: Senior Manager, Lead, Director, Principal, or Head of
- Role involves monetization, pricing, or GTM strategy

## Weak Signals / Penalize (score DOWN for these)
- Role is primarily execution or project management without strategic ownership
- Role is inside a pure Sales, Marketing, or Finance org with no product proximity
- Role is heavily operational (logistics, support ops, etc.)
- Company is not a recognized brand or top-tier startup
- Role title includes "Coordinator," "Analyst" (unless Senior Analyst), or "Associate" without seniority modifier

## Scoring Guide
- 9–10: Perfect match — prestige company, clearly strategic, product-adjacent, right seniority
- 7–8: Strong match — right function but company tier or seniority is slightly off
- 5–6: Moderate match — some strategic elements but mixed with execution or ops-heavy work
- 3–4: Weak match — surface only if nothing better exists that week
- 1–2: Poor match — do not surface
